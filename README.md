## 1. The Kraft Heinz Company (NASDAQ: KHC)
**Source:** 10-K FY2025 (SEC EDGAR) | **Currency:** USD millions | **Standard:** US GAAP

5-year projection (FY2026E–FY2030E) with revenue buildup by geographic segment
(North America, Int'l Developed Markets, Emerging Markets), NWC modeling,
Debt Schedule and CapEx/D&A. All historical data sourced exclusively from p.50,
52, 55 and Note 21 of the 10-K FY2025.

**Key insight:** KHC reported a net loss of $(5,848)mm in FY2025 while
generating $4,462mm in operating cash flow. The gap: $9.3B in non-cash
goodwill and intangible impairments. The business generates cash — the
damage was accounting.

## 2. Loma Negra S.A. (BCBA: LOMA)
**Source:** EEFF Consolidados NIIF | **Currency:** ARS miles | **Standard:** IAS 29

Argentine cement company (5 segments: Cement, Concrete, Aggregates,
Railway, Other). Model handles IAS 29 hyperinflationary accounting including
RECPAM (monetary position result) and FX differences — critical items
for Argentine companies under high inflation.

**Key insight:** FY2024 net income of ARS 202B was driven by a ARS 345B
RECPAM gain — not operations. Normalized EBIT was only ARS 146B.
Projections strip out these non-recurring monetary items and show the
true operational trajectory as Argentina normalizes.
